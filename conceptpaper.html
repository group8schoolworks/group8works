No To Plastic Waste

In our every day community, one of the biggest problems we see is plastic waste. Plastics in streets and canals make the community look dirty and also dangerous because plastics cause massive flooding in canals and drainages, especially when it rains heavily. It also brings diseases like leptospirosis. A lot of people can get that disease by walking in flooded areas, and the most common is dengue, as mosquitoes like watery areas because they lay their eggs there. Our mission as a group is to help protect our community from the dangers of plastics and our vision is to have a community that is clean, safe, and healthy. Because of this, we created the project "No to Plastic, Yes to Paper." The goal is to promote paper products as a better choice since they are easy to dispose of and not harmful to the community. If our community joins together, we can make our community clean and safe again.
